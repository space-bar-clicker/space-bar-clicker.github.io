How to Play Money Clicker

  1. Start by clicking the big dollar button to earn your first Dollars manually.
  2. Once you have 15 Dollars, buy your first Side Hustle upgrade to earn +$1 per second passively.
  3. Continue clicking while your Side Hustle generates income in the background.
  4. At 100 Dollars, unlock the Stock Portfolio for +$5 per second—your first major investment.
  5. Save up 500 Dollars to purchase Real Estate, pumping your passive income to +$20/sec.
  6. Invest in Click Power early to double (then quadruple) your manual earnings per tap.
  7. Unlock the Startup tier at 2,500 Dollars for +$100/sec and watch your wealth snowball.
  8. Reach Billionaire Status at 15,000 Dollars for +$500/sec and become a tycoon.
  9. Use the Save button to preserve your empire across browser sessions.
  10. Press SPACE on your keyboard to click faster without wearing out your mouse!

Strategy Guide

The key to early game dominance is balancing active clicking with passive investments. Do not just save blindly for expensive upgrades—buying multiple cheap Side Hustles often yields better returns per dollar spent than waiting for one big purchase.

Click Power upgrades follow an exponential curve (doubling each level), making them incredibly powerful in the late game. However, early on, every dollar spent on Click Power is a dollar not earning passive income. A good rule of thumb: buy Click Power when you can afford it in under 30 seconds of passive income.

The 1.15x cost scaling means each duplicate upgrade grows more expensive. Use the progress bar below your score to track exactly how close you are to the next affordable upgrade. When multiple upgrades are unlocked, calculate which gives the best "income per cost" ratio—sometimes a second Stock Portfolio beats your first Startup.

AFK strategy matters: before stepping away, dump every dollar into your highest unlocked passive tier. The compounding effect of leaving your tab open with strong passive income often outperforms active clicking over long periods. Reset only when you want to speedrun or test new opening strategies.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Money Clicker?

Money Clicker is a free online idle clicker game where you earn virtual Dollars by clicking and buying passive income upgrades. It is themed around modern wealth-building concepts like side hustles, stocks, and startups.

Is Money Clicker really free?

Yes! Money Clicker is 100% free to play in your browser with no downloads, no registration, and no microtransactions. Just open the page and start clicking.

How do I save my progress?

Click the 💾 Save button to store your progress in your browser's local storage. Your save will persist as long as you do not clear your browser data. Auto-save also runs every 30 seconds.

What is the best upgrade to buy first?

Always start with Side Hustle (15 Dollars) because passive income multiplies your total earnings over time. After that, balance between buying more cheap upgrades and saving for the next tier.

Does clicking faster help?

Absolutely! Each click gives you Dollars equal to your Click Power level. You can also press the SPACE key to click, and building a 10x combo triggers a Critical Hit for double points.

Can I play Money Clicker on mobile?

Yes! The game is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets. Touch the dollar button to click, and swipe through the upgrades panel to browse.

What happens when I reach Billionaire Status?

Billionaire Status gives +$500 per second per owned unit, making it the ultimate passive income source. However, the game continues indefinitely—you can stack multiple Billionaire Status units and push your score into the trillions!

How does the combo system work?

Click rapidly without pausing for more than 2 seconds to build a combo counter. At 10 consecutive clicks, your next click scores a Critical Hit with a 2x multiplier and a dramatic popup!
